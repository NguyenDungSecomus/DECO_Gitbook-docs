# 🍂 What happen after you uninstall DECO ?

**What happens on my side?**

* Your store's theme will remain completely unaffected when you uninstall DECO. No cleanup is required.&#x20;
* Uninstalling simply deactivates the DECO app extension, stopping it from running on your store.

**What happens to my data?**

* All your created labels, badges, banners, and trust badges, along with their settings, are securely saved in your DECO account indefinitely.
* They'll be deactivated but ready for you if you decide to return.

**What about my paid plan?**

* If you're on a paid plan, it will be automatically canceled when you uninstall DECO. So please consider this carefully before uninstalling.

We're always happy to welcome you back! Just reactivate your account to access all your saved creations. They will be waiting here for you forever! 🌸
