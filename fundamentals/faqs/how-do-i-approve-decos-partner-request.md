# How do I approve DECO's partner request ?

{% hint style="info" %}
We need the temporarily access to your store to help you with **settings** or **fix the problem** reported.

We promise to be careful with every setting on your website. When we fix all problems, you can remove the access from your end.
{% endhint %}

#### STEP 1 <a href="#step-1" id="step-1"></a>

Go to the **Setting** on the left bottom panel&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (254).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Please go to **Settings > Users** and click it to check our access request here.<br>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (258).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**STEP 2**

You just need to click the **Accept Request** button to allow DECO access to your store, or even edit the permissions and grant us more access.

**Our request sender: PieLab- which is our brand name**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (259).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (260).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**STEP 3**

After you click **Accept request**, you can see our permission updated as "_**active**_" here

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (261).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once you accepted the request, drop us a message, our technical team can resolve your problem quickly&#x20;
