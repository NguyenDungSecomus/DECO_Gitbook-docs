## 1. Check If DECO Has Been Enabled In Your Theme Yet

Sometimes, your created labels/badges are not showing up on your store is due to the missing of this step. So, please troubleshoot by re-checking the status in the theme editor.

## 2. Issues coming from the Theme

&#x20;Sometimes, theme customizations or third-party apps can affect how DECO displays. This can happen with:

* <mark style="color:blue;">**Custom-code themes**</mark>: If you uploaded the theme, or your theme has unique code for layout or fonts, it might occasionally overlap with DECO.
* <mark style="color:blue;">**Themes modified by page builders or filter apps**</mark>: These apps can sometimes alter your theme's structure, which might impact DECO's integration.
* <mark style="color:blue;">**Special themes**</mark>: While DECO works with most themes, a few might require a small adjustment for optimal display.
