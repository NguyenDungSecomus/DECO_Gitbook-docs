# How To Position Labels Anywhere On Product Images ?

## **Method 1: Using&#x20;**<mark style="color:blue;">**Adjust position manually**</mark>**&#x20;or&#x20;**<mark style="color:blue;">**Edit margin**</mark>

Open the label/badge you want to adjust size > On <mark style="color:blue;">**Design**</mark> tab, navigate to <mark style="color:blue;">**Position**</mark> section > Here, you will see 2 options (as in the screenshot below) > let's <mark style="color:blue;">**select 1 option you prefer**</mark> ( Both of them can help you position your label/badge anywhere on product images)&#x20;

## Method 2: Using Label Bundles

By grouping labels on a bundle, you can move that group anywhere on product images, and don't need to worry about manually adjusting the position

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (269).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (271).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

On products that don't meet conditions of all label in the group, the label will automatically"move up" when there is no label above it, **prevent overlap**, saving you time and ensuring a professional look.
