# How do I update DECO partner's request?

{% hint style="info" %}
We need the temporarily access to your store to help you with **settings** or **fix the problem** reported.

We promise to be careful with every setting on your website. When we fix all problems, you can remove the access from your end.
{% endhint %}

#### STEP 1 <a href="#step-1" id="step-1"></a>

Go to Shopify settings on the bottom left panel

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (241).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**STEP 2**\
Choose Users > Roles. Here, you need to select our PieLab access, and then tick the permissions you need to update before clicking **SAVE**. After that, the role is updated successfully
