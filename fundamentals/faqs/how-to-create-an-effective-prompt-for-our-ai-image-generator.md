# How To Create An Effective Prompt For Our AI Image Generator?

## <mark style="color:blue;">**1. Be Specific and Descriptive:**</mark>

* **Avoid vagueness:** Instead of "a beautiful badge," try "a unique badge, for the campaign: 50% off on all products of my store: Handmade Candle, modern vibe, main color: Black & white, some icons: flame/ sparkle
* **Detail is key:** Specify the subject, vibe, colors, textures, art style, and even the mood or atmosphere you envision.

## <mark style="color:blue;">**2. Use Keywords Strategically:**</mark>

* **Art styles:** Include terms like "impressionism," "photorealism," "art nouveau," "cyberpunk," or "anime" to influence the artistic style.
* **Color:** "pastel colors," "monochromatic," "vibrant," "Gradient colors.", "Pink", "Red",...
* **Text Font: San Serif, Arial, Times New Roman, Lobster,...**
* **Text style: Bold, Italic, Uppercase,...**
* **Content: Free shipping, Sale, Limited time,....**

**Example:**&#x20;

**❌ A free shipping label**

**✅ A free shipping label with "Free Shipping" bold, sans-serif text and a truck icon in flat vector design style, impressionism**

## <mark style="color:blue;">**3. Explore Artistic Techniques and Inspiration:**</mark>

* **Reference artists:** "In the style of Van Gogh," "inspired by Alphonse Mucha."
* **Specific art movements:** "Art Deco architecture," "Surrealist painting."
* **Photography styles:** "National Geographic photography," "fashion photography."
* **Film and cinematography:** "Wes Anderson color palette," "film noir lighting."

## <mark style="color:blue;">**4. Iterate and Refine:**</mark>

* **Start simple, then add complexity:** Begin with a basic prompt and gradually add details based on the initial results.
* **Try variations:** Experiment with different keywords, styles, and parameters.

**Example:**&#x20;

**❌ An  Easter sale badge with a round shape**

**✅ Draw a pink sale badge, with a round shape, the content: Only Today in the middle and bold, font: San Serif, for Easter sale**
