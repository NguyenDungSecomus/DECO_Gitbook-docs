## STEP 1: Design **✨**

#### 1. Customize The Badge Design

* Here, you can select the _**fill type, background color, and shape**_ to your preference.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (610).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
<mark style="color:blue;">**NOTE**</mark>: If you want to _**upload your pre-designed shape**_, let's choose: **"Background"** >> Select: **"Upload..."**
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (611).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### 2. Select The Badge Position

First, choose display location:

* <mark style="color:blue;">**Inside product image**</mark>: The badge will _**overlay on**_ the product image
* <mark style="color:blue;">**Outside product image**</mark>: The badge will _**appear outside**_ the product image

Then, depending on your choice above, you'll see different positioning options:

<div><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (614).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/outside image.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

#### 3. Edit The Badge Content

* In this step, you can type anything you want in the badge content, change the text font, or add translations for the content.
* Or if you want a dynamic content, select "_**AutoText**_" and select a suitable content.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (615).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="warning" %}
Please take a look at important notes about "AutoText" [here](../../our-special-features/add-dynamic-content.md)\
and mandatory rules for badge text [here](../../fundamentals/faqs/mandatory-rules-for-text-label-text-badge-banner-content.md) before saving your badge.
{% endhint %}

#### 4. Adjust The Badge Size

👉 Edit Shape Size:&#x20;

Choose 1 in 2 size options:

* <mark style="color:blue;">**Same size on all screens**</mark>: Width and height _**remain consistent**_ across _**all devices and pages**_

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (618).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* <mark style="color:blue;">**Custom size per screen**</mark>: Set _**different dimensions**_ for _**each device**_ (Desktop/Mobile) and _**page type**_ (Collection/Product page)

**Size Units:** Choose between Percentage (%) or Pixels (px)

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (619).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
**NOTE**: Size settings for _**Collection Pages**_ automatically apply to _**Home Page and Search Page.**_
{% endhint %}

👉 Edit Text Font Size:

* <mark style="color:blue;">**Check for the box "Auto-adjust text size"**</mark>: Text size _**adjusts automatically**_ in _**percentage (%)**_ to fit within the shape
* <mark style="color:blue;">**Unchecked for the box "Auto-adjust text size"**</mark>: Set a _**fixed font size**_ in _**pixels (px)**_ for precise control.

<div><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (620).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/untick- auto adjust box.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

#### 5. Choose Display Devices

Tick to select which devices will show the badge:&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (621).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### 6. Customize Other Adjustments & Additional Settings For The Badge&#x20;

You can add border, round corner, add animation,... or use CSS code to make your badge look more stunning!

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (622).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## STEP 2: Products 🏷️

_(_&#x44;efine which products should display your badge&#x73;_)_

### Option I -  Specific products&#x20;

* **Manually select** individual products to showcase your badge.&#x20;

⇒ Choosing this option ensures the badge _**appears only on the selected items.**_

{% hint style="info" %}
**NOTE**: The app _**can't detect**_ products _**in draft/archived status OR not published on the Sale channel**_
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (657).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (624).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Option II - All products&#x20;

* With 1-click, the badge will be applied to all active products.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (658).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Option III - Automated Conditions

<details>

<summary>Automatic conditions, which save much of your time</summary>



</details>

NOTE: Before selecting any conditions, please choose the matching condition first:&#x20;

* <mark style="color:blue;">**All conditions**</mark>: The badge will _<mark style="color:$info;">**ONLY APPEAR ON PRODUCTS**</mark>_ that _<mark style="color:$info;">**MEET EVERY SINGLE CONDITION**</mark>_ you selected in the app.
* <mark style="color:blue;">**Any conditions**</mark>: The badge _<mark style="color:$info;">**WILL APPEAR ON PRODUCTS**</mark>_ that _<mark style="color:$info;">**MEET**</mark>**&#x20;AT LEAST 1 OF THE CONDITIONS**_ you selected in the app.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (659).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

👉 Example:&#x20;

Say you select these conditions:

* Product collection = "Electronics"
* In stock

**🛠️ With "All conditions":** A product must be in the Electronics collections **AND** be in stock to show the badge.

**🛠️ With "Any conditions":** A product **only needs to meet one requirement**—it could belong to the collection: Electronics **OR** be in stock to show the badge.

### ⚙️Basic Conditions

You can set up base on:

* The weight range,&#x20;
* The price range
* The product title&#x20;

&#x20;<mark style="color:blue;">⇒</mark> Y**our badge will automatically detect & show on products meeting the requirements.**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (660).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### 🔁 Advanced Conditions

#### 1. Product collections

* Click "_**Select collections**_" and choose the specific collections where you want the badge to appear.

**⇒** The badge will automatically display on _**all products within selected collections.**_

<div><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (626).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_3 (4).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

#### 2. Product tags

* Enter your Shopify product tag(s) in the box, and click _**"Add"**_

⇒ The badge will automatically appear on _**all products assigned to these tags.**_

{% hint style="warning" %}
**NOTE**: Remember to select 1 meeting condition: At least one OR All
{% endhint %}

<div><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (627).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/tag condition.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

#### 3. Discount

* Click _**"Select discount"**_, then _**choose a pre-existing discount**_ from your Shopify admin in the list and click _**"Save"**_&#x20;

⇒ The badge will &#x61;_**utomatically appear on all products that meet the specific conditions of that discount.**_

<div><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (629).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/discount select 1.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (630).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### 4. Compare-at price

Choose either _**"Fixed value"**_ OR _**"Value range"**_ then enter your desired amount.

⇒ The badge will display on products that fall within your specified discount criteria.

<div><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (631).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/value range.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

**NOTE**: Don't forget to _**click on the gear icon**_ to select the discount setting.

* _**Select 1 type**_ of status, then _**click on the gear icon**_ to set up the inventory condition.

⇒ The badge will _**display only on products meeting**_ the condition.

#### 9. Include products

* If you've selected the "Product collections" condition but want to _<mark style="color:blue;">**also**</mark>_<mark style="color:blue;">**&#x20;**</mark><mark style="color:blue;">**display the badge on additional products outside those collections**</mark>, use this condition to specify them.

#### 10. Exclude products

* To <mark style="color:blue;">**exclude specific products**</mark><mark style="color:blue;">**&#x20;**</mark>_<mark style="color:blue;">**within**</mark>_<mark style="color:blue;">**&#x20;**</mark><mark style="color:blue;">**selected collections**</mark> from the badge display, use this condition.

#### 11. Add a matching metafield

(_This will help connect your <mark style="color:blue;">**PRODUCT METAFIELD**</mark> with the <mark style="color:blue;">**DECO's BADGE**</mark> >> Then, <mark style="color:blue;">**show the badge**</mark> on <mark style="color:blue;">**products**</mark> with the <mark style="color:blue;">**matching value**</mark>_)

* First, click on the button "_**+ Add metafield**_" to start connecting

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (641).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Next, a list of all product metafields in your store will appear, _**check the corresponding box**_ to select >> Then, _**click "Select"**_ button

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (642).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* After, **select either:**

<mark style="color:blue;">**Exists**</mark>: Show on **all products** that have the **metafield value filled in** (any value).

<mark style="color:blue;">**Equal to**</mark>: Show **only** on products with **the exact value entered**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (643).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

For example:&#x20;

<mark style="color:blue;">**↪ Selected option: Exists**</mark>

* _Metafield key_: `Plant type`
* _Option_: **Exists**
* _Result_: The badge will show on every product that has **any** `Plant type` value (e.g., "Indoor Only", "Outdoor", "Shade Loving", etc.).

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Group 1321315058.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<mark style="color:blue;">**↪  Selected option: Equal to**</mark>

* Metafield key: `Plant type`
* Option: **Equal to**
* Value entered: `Indoor Only`
* Result: The label will show **only** on products with `Plant type = Indoor Only`.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Group 1321315060.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<mark style="color:blue;">**→**</mark> Besides, if you want to <mark style="color:blue;">**show the value of the metafield on the badge**</mark>, please visit [this metafield's common use cases](../../our-special-features/product-metafields/) 🥰

## STEP 3: DISPLAY 📅

_(This is the place to set up the display condition for your badge)_

#### 1. Page display

* Check for pages you would like to display the badge

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (172).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="warning" %}
For other pages such as pages from Themes, and other pages built by other page builder apps(PageFly, Ecomposer,...), kindly contact us via the **live chat** for the quickest setup.
{% endhint %}

#### 2. Show badge on store languages

* Select which languages you would like the badge to display by choosing either:&#x20;

1/ <mark style="color:blue;">**All Languages**</mark>: The badge will display whatever the language is&#x20;

2/ <mark style="color:blue;">**Custom languages**</mark>: Tick for the languages in the DECO app, and the badge will _**ONLY**_ display when customers choose the corresponding language on the website

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (57).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (58).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### 3. Customer country

Here, you can either select to _**display/NOT display**_ your badge _**for customers from selected countries**_

⇒ **The app will&#x20;**_<mark style="color:blue;">**base on the IP address of customers to display/NOT display the label**</mark>_.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (653).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

👉 Example,

When you select _**"Include"**_ + Choose 3 countries: _**Belgium, Bermuda & Brazil**_&#x20;

⇒ Then only _**customers coming from these 3 countries can see the badge**_.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (654).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### 4. Customer condition

Choose _**which types of customers**_ can see the badge:

* By choosing the start date & end date, you can _**schedule when your badge will appear on your website**_.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (175).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* By typing the number in the box, it helps _**control the display order of the badge**_ when _**multiple badges are applied to the same item**_

⇒ _**Preventing overlap**_ and ensuring _**high-priority badges remain visible**_.
