# 🥑 Badge Group

## How Badge Group Works

* <mark style="color:blue;">**Without Badge Group**</mark>\
  &#x20;Badges are arranged in a vertical layout, leaving too much unnecessary space.
* <mark style="color:blue;">**With Badge Group**</mark>\
  &#x20;Badges automatically arrange themselves horizontally, making your product cards look cleaner and more organized.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (214).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Step-by-Step Setup Guide

### ✨Step 1: Create Individual Badges

* Design your badges first **( Skip this step if you already have badges created )**

<mark style="color:blue;">**(Or please take a look at the short video below to know where to create a DECO badge)**</mark>

{% embed url="https://www.loom.com/share/148f35ab35f348d3a78995e084ecf33d?sid=0d3fec1a-41e2-4993-9a91-0c1b94084091" %}

**Example:**

* "Organic"
* "Coming soon"
* "Cruelty Free"
* "100% cotton"



### ✨Step 2: Access Badge Group

* Navigate to the _**"Badge group"**_ section in your DECO dashboard >> **Click "**_**Create group"**_**&#x20;>> Choose&#x20;**_**"Badge group"**_

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (179).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### ✨Step 3: Add Badges to Group

* **On the tab "Select badges" >> Click on "Add badges to group"** >> Select the badges you want to group together

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (180).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Tick to choose badges >> Click: _**"Done**_" button to save the order&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (181).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
**Important:** The selection order determines the display order
{% endhint %}

### ✨Step 4: Customize Design

1. _<mark style="color:blue;">**Go to the "Design" tab >> Choose badge position:**</mark>_

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (182).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. _<mark style="color:blue;">**Select the predefined position**</mark>_

* Left
* Center
* Right

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (183).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. _<mark style="color:blue;">**Choose the display type**</mark>_

* Vertical&#x20;
* Horizontal

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (184).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4. _<mark style="color:blue;">**Set up other customization settings**</mark>_

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (559).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### ✨Step 5: Preview & Publish

1. Click **"Save"** to apply changes
2. Preview your group on your store
3. Make adjustments if needed
4. Publish when satisfied
