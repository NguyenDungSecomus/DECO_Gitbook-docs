### III - Conditions

<details>

<summary>Automatic conditions, which save much of your time</summary>



</details>

NOTE: Before selecting any conditions, please choose the matching setting first:

* **All conditions**: The badge will <mark style="color:blue;">**ONLY APPEAR ON PRODUCTS**</mark> that <mark style="color:blue;">**SATISFY EVERY SINGLE CONDITION**</mark> you selected in the app.
* **Any conditions**: The badge <mark style="color:blue;">**WILL APPEAR ON PRODUCTS**</mark> that <mark style="color:blue;">**SATISFY AT LEAST 1 OF THE CONDITIONS**</mark> you selected in the app.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (73).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

👉 Example:

Say you select these conditions:

* Product collection = "Electronics"
* In stock

**🛠️ With "All conditions":** A product must be in the Electronics collections **AND** be in stock to show the badge.

**🛠️ With "Any conditions":** A product **only needs to meet one requirement**—it could belong to the collection: Electronics **OR** be in stock to show the badge.

#### 11. Add a matching metafield

(_This will help connect your <mark style="color:blue;">**PRODUCT METAFIELD**</mark> with the <mark style="color:blue;">**DECO's BADGE**</mark> >> Then, <mark style="color:blue;">**show the badge**</mark> on <mark style="color:blue;">**products**</mark> with the <mark style="color:blue;">**matching value**</mark>_)

* First, click on the button "_**+ Add metafield**_" to start connecting

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (87).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Next, a list of all product metafields in your store will appear, tick to select >> click **"Select"** button

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (138).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Then, **choose** between these 2 options:&#x20;

<mark style="color:blue;">**Exists**</mark>: Show on **all products** that have the **metafield value filled in** (any value).

<mark style="color:blue;">**Equal to**</mark>: Show **only** on products with **the exact value entered**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (139).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

For example:&#x20;

<mark style="color:blue;">**↪ Exists**</mark>

* _Metafield key_: `Plant type`
* _Option_: **Exists**
* _Result_: The label will show on every product that has **any** `Plant type` value (e.g., "Indoor Only", "Outdoor", "Shade Loving", etc.).

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Group 1321315058.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<mark style="color:blue;">**↪ Equal to**</mark>

* Metafield key: `Plant type`
* Option: **Equal to**
* Value entered: `Indoor Only`
* Result: The label will show **only** on products with `Plant type = Indoor Only`.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Group 1321315060.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<mark style="color:blue;">**→**</mark> Besides, if you want to <mark style="color:blue;">**show the value of the metafield on the label**</mark>, please visit [this metafield's common use cases](../../our-special-features/product-metafields/) 🥰
