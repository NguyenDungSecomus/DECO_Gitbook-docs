# ⛳ DECO Product Badges

## What is a DECO Product Badge? 💥

**A Badge** is any icons/ graphic elements positioned <mark style="color:blue;">**OUTSIDE**</mark> of product images, such as: Under the product title, Below the product price,...

## Where can I create my badges? 🎐

From the DECO homepage >> Click **"Create badge"**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/create new badge.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Then choose 1 badge type to start:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/choose type-badge.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<table data-full-width="false"><thead><tr><th align="center">IMAGE BADGE</th><th align="center">TEXT BADGE</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td align="center">Upload your pre-made design/ Choose from our template gallery.</td><td align="center">Customize the content(e.g. text, shape, color, etc)/ Upload your pre-made design.</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="info" %}
<mark style="color:blue;">**NOTE**</mark>: Once you selected the badge type (Image/Text) in this screen, you couldn't change the badge type in the next step (if you would like to change, you need to create a new badge)
{% endhint %}
