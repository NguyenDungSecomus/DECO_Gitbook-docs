# List of Values Metafield

## ✅ Preparations

Before starting, you need:

* [x] DECO Plan: Growth/Unlimited&#x20;
* [x] Product metafields created in your Shopify admin
* [x] Metafield values assigned to your products

Currently, we support Choice List and Single line text:&#x20;

## ✨Step-by-step Setup

&#x20;<mark style="color:blue;">**Step 1: Choose metafields option**</mark>&#x20;

* Open DECO app >> Navigate to: Labels & Badges then:&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (4) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<mark style="color:blue;">🔹</mark> <mark style="color:blue;"></mark><mark style="color:blue;">**Step 2: Choose the metafield**</mark>&#x20;

After clicking on Add metafields, now you can choose the metafield:&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (5) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<mark style="color:blue;">🔹</mark> <mark style="color:blue;"></mark><mark style="color:blue;">**Step 3: Select Options**</mark>&#x20;

After clicking on Add metafields, now you can choose one of these options:&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (7) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* The "Exists" condition specifies that our badges will be displayed as long as the specific metafield is present on the product, regardless of its specific value.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (8) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* The "Include" condition specifies that our badges will be displayed only when the product contains the specific metafield value defined in your settings.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (10) (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* The "Match all of" condition specifies that our badges will only display when the product contains every one of the defined values

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (11).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* The "Match at least one of" condition specifies that our badges will display when the product contains at least one of the defined values&#x20;
