# 🪴 Add Translations For Content

## 🎐 Which Features Have This Translation Feature

* Badges
* Banners&#x20;
* Trust Badges
* Tooltips

## ✨ Step 1: Preparations - Set Up Languages in Shopify

_<mark style="color:$primary;">**(Skip this step if you've already configured multiple languages on your store.)**</mark>_

#### 1/ Access Language Settings

* Navigate to _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Settings**</mark>_ in the bottom-left corner of your _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Shopify admin**</mark>_

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (19).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### 2/ Add Your Desired Languages

* Click on _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Languages**</mark>_ >> _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Add a language**</mark>_
* _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Select the language(s)**</mark>_ you want to display on your website
* Click _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Next**</mark>_ to proceed

<div><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (20).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (21).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (22).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### 3/ Enable and Publish

* Toggle the button to enable each language
* Click **Done**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (23).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
**Important:** Your new languages will be in the status _<mark style="color:blue;">**"Not published"**</mark>_ —don't forget to _<mark style="color:blue;">**publish them to make them live on your store**</mark>_
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (24).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## ✨Step 2: Processing - On the DECO app

⇒ Now for the final step: Select your language(s) and add translations for your DECO badges, banners, and trust badges.

{% hint style="success" %}
Adding translations is <mark style="color:blue;">**available for all**</mark> DECO features, including: <mark style="color:blue;">**BADGE, BANNER, TRUST BADGE**</mark>
{% endhint %}

### 1. Access Created Badges/Banners/Trust Badges

* _<mark style="color:blue;">**Open Created Badges (Only Available For TEXT BADGE)**</mark>_

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (25).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* _<mark style="color:blue;">**Open Created Banner**</mark>_

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (26).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* _<mark style="color:blue;">**Open Created Trust Badge**</mark>_

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (27).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### 2. Add Translations For Content

_<mark style="color:blue;">**To provide a localized experience, follow the steps below for each feature type.**</mark>_

#### 🏅 For Badges:&#x20;

* Click the _<mark style="color:$primary;">**pencil icon**</mark>_ to _<mark style="color:$primary;">**open**</mark>_ your selected badge.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (28).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Navigate to the _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Design tab**</mark>_ and find the _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Content**</mark>_ section.
* Click _<mark style="color:$primary;">**"Add translations"**</mark>_ and _<mark style="color:$primary;">**select**</mark>_ your _<mark style="color:$primary;">**target language.**</mark>_
* _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Enter**</mark>_ _<mark style="color:$primary;">**your translated text**</mark>_ and click "_<mark style="color:$primary;">**Add"**</mark>_.

<div><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (30).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (31).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

#### 🚩 For Banners:&#x20;

* Click the _<mark style="color:$primary;">**pencil icon**</mark>_ to _<mark style="color:$primary;">**open**</mark>_ your selected banner.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (32).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* In the _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Design tab**</mark>_, scroll to the bottom to find the _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Translation**</mark>_ section.
* Click _<mark style="color:$primary;">**"Add translations"**</mark>**,**_ select a language, and enter your text.
* Click _<mark style="color:$primary;">**"Add"**</mark>_ to save.

<div><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (33).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (34).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

#### 🛡️ For Trust Badges:

* Click the _<mark style="color:$primary;">**pencil icon**</mark>_ to _<mark style="color:$primary;">**open**</mark>_ your selected trust badge.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (35).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* In the _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Design tab**</mark>_, scroll to the bottom to find the _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Translation**</mark>_ section.
* Click _<mark style="color:$primary;">**"Add translations"**</mark>**,&#x20;**<mark style="color:$primary;">**select your language**</mark>_, and _<mark style="color:$primary;">**type the translation**</mark>_.
* Click _<mark style="color:$primary;">**"Add"**</mark>_ to finish.

<div><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (36).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (38).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

#### 🛡️ For Tooltips:&#x20;

Click the _<mark style="color:$primary;">**pencil icon**</mark>_ to _<mark style="color:$primary;">**open**</mark>_ your selected badge.<br>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (683).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

In the _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Design tab**</mark>_, scroll to the bottom to find the _<mark style="color:$primary;">**Other Settings**</mark>_ section.

* Here choose _<mark style="color:purple;">**Show tooltip for badge**</mark>_
* Click on _<mark style="color:purple;">**Add translations**</mark>_

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (684).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Here you can _**select your language**_ and type the _**tooltip**_ that you want.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (686).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You can _**add as many translations**_ as you want and also _**edit**_ them here:&#x20;

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (688).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
