# 🤝 Customer Conditions

## How It Works

⇒ If you sell B2B or wholesale and have different customer groups, this feature is for you.&#x20;

⇒ It allows you to _<mark style="color:blue;">**create targeted promotions and experiences for each customer segment**</mark>_, helping you serve different groups more effectively.

## Detail Steps

### ✨ Step 1: Open An Existing Badge

* Navigate to: _<mark style="color:blue;">**"Label & Badge"**</mark>_ in the navigation menu >> Click on the _<mark style="color:blue;">**pencil icon**</mark>_ to open any badges

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (15).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### ✨ Step 2: Set Up The Customer Conditions

* Then you'll see this screen, go to: "Display" tab >> Scroll down to "Customer condition" section. Choose one of the following options:

1/ <mark style="color:blue;">**All customers**</mark>: The badge will be _<mark style="color:$primary;">**visible to everyone**</mark>_ visiting your store.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (16).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2/ <mark style="color:blue;">**Customer with tags**</mark>: The badge will be _<mark style="color:$primary;">**visible or hidden**</mark>_ based on _<mark style="color:$primary;">**specific customer tags**</mark>_.

3/ <mark style="color:blue;">**Logged-in customers**</mark>: The badge will _<mark style="color:$primary;">**only appear**</mark>_ to customers who are _<mark style="color:$primary;">**signed into their accounts**</mark><mark style="color:$primary;">.</mark>_
