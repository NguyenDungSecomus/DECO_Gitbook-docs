# 🧭 Countdown Timer

First, if you're a new user, create your first banner by clicking on "Banners" > choose "Create banner"

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (294).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

This is the feature for "Fixed banner" only

## 1. How to find it?

Then you will see this screen: choose "Fixed banner" > Scroll down to "Countdown Timer" section as in this screenshot:

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (474).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## 2. Custom Your Countdown Timer

<details>

<summary>Step 1: To start off, tick for the box: "Show countdown on banner", you will have a result like this: </summary>



</details>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (466).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<details>

<summary>Step 2: Choose and adjust 1 in 2 time types:</summary>



</details>

### Count down to a specific date:

> You can choose the date, the month, the year and even the time for your countdown, so that your timer will start to count down from the time you entered



<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (470).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Fixed times (Minute):

> When you enter 1 specific number, the timer will start to count down from that time

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (471).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
Note: with "Fixed times( Minute), you can loop your countdown as well, so that when the time on banner end, it will begin again automatically.
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (295).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<details>

<summary>Step 3: Enter the text you want for 4 time labels(optional)</summary>



</details>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (312).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<details>

<summary>Step 4: Choose action for your banner, when the time ends: <mark style="background-color:green;"><strong>Do nothing</strong></mark> OR <mark style="background-color:green;">Hide banner</mark></summary>



</details>

* **Do nothing:** Banner will **STAY to DISPLAY** on your store even though the countdown time ended
* **Hide banner**: Banner will **STOP to DISPLAY** on your store when the countdown time ended and your banner status will be automatically changed into: "**Expired"** in-app
