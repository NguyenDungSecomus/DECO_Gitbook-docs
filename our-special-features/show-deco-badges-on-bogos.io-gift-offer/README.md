# 🌵 Show DECO Badges On BOGOS.io Gift Offer

## Before You Start ✨

✅ You need an **active BOGOS Gift Offer** (not other offer types). If you haven't installed BOGOS yet, [\[install here\]](https://apps.shopify.com/freegifts?utm_source=Partner\&utm_medium=DECO\&utm_campaign=Integration_Feature_Gitbook)\
✅ You need a **DECO Badge** created

## Step 1: Prepare Your BOGOS Gift Offer 🎁

### If you don't have a gift offer yet:

1. Go to BOGOS **Home page**
2. Click **"Create offer"**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (251).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. Select **"Gift offer"** type
4. Complete your offer setup

{% hint style="warning" %}
Only the "Gift Offer" type can work with DECO's labels & badges
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (249).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### If you already have gift offers

1. Go to **"All offers"** page
2. Select & copy the **title** of your active gift offers
3. Make sure they're **"Gift"** type and **"Active"** status

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (250).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Step 2: Connect DECO Badges to BOGOS 🔖

### Connect the existing DECO label:

* Open the _<mark style="color:blue;">**DECO app**</mark>_ >> Then, navigate to _<mark style="color:blue;">**"Label & Badge"**</mark>_ in the navigation menu >> _<mark style="color:blue;">**Click**</mark>_ on the _<mark style="color:blue;">**pencil icon**</mark>_ to edit any badges

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (678).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Next, move to the <mark style="color:blue;">**"Products**</mark>**"** tab >> Scroll to _<mark style="color:blue;">**"Integrations"**</mark>_ section >> Then, click _<mark style="color:blue;">**"^"**</mark>_ button next to _<mark style="color:blue;">**"Bundle & Free gift apps"**</mark>_

<div><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (679).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (680).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* Hence, you'll see the app: BOGOS.io Free Gift appear, click _<mark style="color:blue;">**"Select"**</mark>_

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (681).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Step 3: Link Your BOGOS Offers

* Click on the search box, _<mark style="color:blue;">**all active Bogos gift offers**</mark>_ will _<mark style="color:blue;">**appear in the list**</mark>_
* Check the box to select.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (234).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
**NOTE**: If you did selected products for your BOGOS offer, <mark style="color:blue;">**no need to re-select**</mark> them in DECO!&#x20;
{% endhint %}

4. Click <mark style="color:blue;">**"Save"**</mark>**&#x20;to finish setting up**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (682).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### ✅ Done!

Your DECO badges will <mark style="color:blue;">**now appear on products having the BOGOS gift offers!**</mark>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (235).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Quick Troubleshooting

* **Badge not showing?** → Check if your BOGOS offer is "Active" and type "Gift"
* **Can't find the offer?** → Make sure you copied the exact offer title
* **Wrong products?** → Check if you have also selected other products for your DECO badges
